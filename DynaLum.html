Dynamite Lumpia


Dynamite Lumpia also known as Lumpiang Dinamita, is a fiery Filipino appetizer that packs a punch. It features long green chilies stuffed with a savory filling, typically ground pork and cheese,then wrapped in lumpia wrappers and deep-fried until golden brown and crispy.



Ingredients:

For the Filling:

  • 1/2 lb. ground pork

  • 1/4 cup chopped onion

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions

  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese (or your preferred cheese)

  • 1/2 Salt and pepper to taste

      • For the Lumpia:

    • 12-15 long green chilies (siling haba or similar)
    • 12-15 lumpia wrappers
    • Cooking oil for deep frying
    • For the Dipping Sauce (optional):
    • Sweet chili sauce
    • Banana ketchup
    • Spiced vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Filling: :In a pan, heat a tablespoon of oil over medium heat. Sauté garlic and onion until fragrant. Add ground pork and cook until browned. Season with salt and pepper. Remove from heat and let cool slightly. Combine the cooked pork with chopped green onions and shredded cheese. Mix well.


  2. Stuff the Chilies: Slice each chili lengthwise, leaving the stem intact. Carefully remove the seeds and membranes. Stuff the chilies with the pork filling, using a spoon or piping bag if you have one.


  3. Wrap the Lumpia: Place a lumpia wrapper on a flat surface. Fold one corner over slightly, leaving room for the chili stem. Place the stuffed chili on the wrapper, with the stem pointing out. Fold the opposite side over the chili and roll tightly, sealing the edge with a dab of water. Repeat with the remaining chilies.


  4. Deep Fry: Fry the lumpia in batches for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and crispy. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels.


  5. Serve:Serve the Dynamite Lumpia hot with your preferred dipping sauce. Enjoy!


    6. Tips and Variations::

    • Chili Peppers: You can use other types of green chilies, such as jalapeños or serrano peppers, but adjust the quantity based on your heat preference.


    • Filling: Feel free to experiment with different cheese varieties or add other ingredients to the filling, such as chopped vegetables or a bit of soy sauce.


    • Dipping Sauce: Consider making your own dipping sauce with a combination of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and chili flakes.


    • Air Fryer: You can also air fry Dynamite Lumpia for a healthier option. Preheat your air fryer to 375°F (190°C) and cook for 10-12 minutes, flipping halfway through.
