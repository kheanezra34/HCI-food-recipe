Chicken Arroz Caldo


Chicken Arroz Caldo , is a comforting Filipino rice porridge, perfect for chilly days.



Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs bone-in, skin-on chicken pieces (thighs and drumsticks recommended)

  • 1 cup glutinous rice (malagkit), rinsed

  • 1 cup jasmine rice, rinsed (or substitute with another long-grain white rice)

  • 6-8 cups chicken broth (homemade is best, but store-bought low-sodium works)

  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil

  • 1 large onion, chopped

  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed

  • 4 thumbs ginger, minced

  • 2 tablespoons kasubha (dried safflower) - optional, for color (or a pinch of saffron)

  • 12-3 tablespoons fish sauce (patis), to taste

  • Ground black pepper, to taste

  • Garnishes:

  • 1/2 cup chopped scallions

  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, sliced

  • 1/2 cup toasted garlic (see instructions below)

  • 1 lemon or 4 calamansi wedges/li>

Instructions:

  1. Toast the Garlic: Heat a small amount of oil in a pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook, stirring frequently, until golden brown and crispy. Remove and set aside on a paper towel-lined plate.


  2. Sauté Aromatics: Heat cooking oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, crushed garlic, and minced ginger. Sauté until softened and fragrant, about 3-5 minutes.


  3. Brown the Chicken: Add the chicken pieces to the pot and cook until lightly browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.


  4. Add Rice: Add both the glutinous and jasmine rice to the pot. Stir and cook for 1 minute.


  5. Add Broth and Simmer: Pour in 6 cups of chicken broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 35-40 minutes, or until the rice is cooked through and the broth has thickened to your desired consistency. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking. Add more broth if needed.


  6. Season and Finish: Stir in the kasubha (or saffron), fish sauce, and black pepper. Cook for another 3-5 minutes


  7. Serve: Ladle the arroz caldo into bowls. Top generously with chopped scallions, sliced hard-boiled eggs, toasted garlic, and lemon or calamansi wedges. Serve hot.
