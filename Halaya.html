Ube Halaya


Ube Halaya, is a sweet, creamy jam made from purple yam, milk, and sugar. It's a versatile dessert that can be enjoyed on its own, used as a spread, or incorporated into other desserts like cakes and ice cream.



Ingredients:

  • 1 lb purple yam, peeled and cubed

  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk

  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Boil the cubed purple yam in water until tender, about 15-20 minutes.


  2. Drain the yam and mash it thoroughly.


  3. In a saucepan, combine the mashed yam, condensed milk, evaporated milk, butter, and salt.


  4. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and becomes smooth, about 15-20 minutes.


  5. Let cool completely before serving.
